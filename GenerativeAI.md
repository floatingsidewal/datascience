# Generative AI & Large Language Models

Graduate programs in Generative AI focus on cutting-edge generative models, transformer architectures, prompt engineering, and responsible AI deployment. This rapidly evolving field is at the forefront of AI innovation.

---

## What This Subdiscipline Studies

Generative AI encompasses models that create new content including text, images, audio, and code. Graduate programs cover:

- **Large Language Models (LLMs)**: GPT, Claude, LLaMA, transformer architectures
- **Multimodal Learning**: Vision-language models, audio-visual generation, cross-modal transfer
- **Fine-Tuning Techniques**: RLHF, LoRA, QLoRA, instruction tuning
- **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)**: Knowledge retrieval, vector databases, context augmentation
- **Prompt Engineering**: Chain-of-thought, few-shot learning, prompt optimization
- **Responsible AI**: Bias mitigation, alignment, safety, ethical deployment
- **LLM Systems**: Efficient inference, model compression, deployment at scale

---

## Real-World Applications

### Content Creation
AI assistants help write marketing copy, articles, code, and creative content. Businesses use LLMs for automated report generation and documentation.

### Customer Service
Intelligent chatbots powered by LLMs handle customer inquiries, provide product information, and resolve issues with human-like conversation.

### Software Development
AI coding assistants suggest code completions, explain code, generate tests, and help debug issues. Tools like GitHub Copilot transform development workflows.

### Legal & Finance
Document summarization, contract analysis, regulatory compliance checking, and financial report generation leverage LLM capabilities.

### Healthcare
Medical AI assistants help with documentation, literature review, patient education, and clinical decision support.

CMU's School of Computer Science offers a 100% online Graduate Certificate specifically focused on Generative AI and LLMs. The 12-month program provides cutting-edge training from expert faculty.

**Key Features**
- 100% online format for working professionals
- Taught by CMU's nationally-ranked School of Computer Science
- Three graduate-level, credit-bearing courses
- Live online sessions once weekly (evening)

**Curriculum Tracks**
1. **Theory**: Large Language Models: Methods and Applications
2. **Data Representation**: Multimodal Machine Learning
3. **Scalability**: Large Language Model Systems

**Topics Covered**
- Efficient fine-tuning and RLHF
- GPU acceleration and hardware optimization
- Model compression for deployment
- Multimodal systems (audio-visual, image generation, video captioning)
- Online system maintenance

**Admission Requirements**
- Undergraduate degree in CS, ML, data science, or related field
- Proficiency in applied math and statistics
- Python programming expertise
- ML and deep learning experience

**Next Start**: Fall 2026 (Priority deadline: January 28, 2026)
